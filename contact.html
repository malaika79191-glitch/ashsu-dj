Coinbase Support - Official Website

Coinbase is a cryptocurrency exchange platform where you can buy, sell, trade, and store digital assets like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and others. Coinbase offers a user-friendly interface that is suitable for both new and seasoned traders. It provides a number of tools and services, such as a digital asset wallet, a trading platform, and educational materials, to help users in understanding and navigating the world of cryptocurrency.

How to Log In to Coinbase App

  1. Open the Coinbase app and tap “Sign In”.
  2. Enter your email.
  3. Enter your password.
  4. Verify your identity (if prompted).
  5. Confirm your email (if using a new device or location).
